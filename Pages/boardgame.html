Description

This campaign aimed to address the rising issue of teenage vaping. The strategy focused on educating younger children before entering secondary school. This board game would educate them about the health risks associated with vaping. By fostering early awareness, the initiative sought to reduce the likelihood of vaping experimentation in later years.
